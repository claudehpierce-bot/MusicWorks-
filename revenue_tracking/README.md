# Revenue Tracking
**System:** MindSpark MusicWorks™

This folder holds revenue tracking templates, monthly reports, and analytics summaries for each release.

## What Goes Here

- Revenue stream activation checklists (from Agent 12)
- KPI dashboard templates (from Agent 12)
- Monthly revenue reports
- Distributor payout records (copies)
- PRO royalty statements (copies)
- SoundExchange distribution records
- Annual revenue summaries

## Subfolder Structure Per Project

```
/revenue_tracking/
  /becoming_vol_1/
    revenue_activation_checklist.md
    kpi_dashboard_template.md
    monthly_reports/
      2026_09_month1.md
      2026_10_month2.md
      [...]
    annual_summary_2026.md
```

## Reporting Cadence

| Report | Frequency | Who Pulls It | Who Reviews It |
|--------|-----------|--------------|----------------|
| Streaming metrics | Weekly (first month), Monthly (after) | Team / Founder | Founder |
| Distributor revenue | Monthly | Founder | Founder |
| PRO royalties | Quarterly | Founder | Founder |
| SoundExchange | Semi-annual | Founder | Founder |
| Full revenue summary | Annual | Founder | Founder + Accountant |

## Principle

Gospel music and sound financial stewardship are not in conflict. Tracking revenue accurately allows MindSpark MusicWorks™ to reinvest in better music, better ministry, and eventually to serve more artists. This folder exists to keep the business side as accountable as the spiritual side.
