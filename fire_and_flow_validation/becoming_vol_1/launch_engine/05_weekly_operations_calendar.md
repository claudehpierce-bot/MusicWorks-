## CONTENT CREATION RULES (For All Weeks)

These rules govern every piece of content in the campaign:

1. **No post goes out without a human reading it first.** Even scheduled posts should be verified before they fire.
2. **Every post that references scripture must quote it accurately.** Check the translation. Check the verse number. Never quote from memory.
3. **Never manufacture urgency.** "Stream NOW before it's gone" does not apply to music that will live on platforms indefinitely. Do not use pressure tactics.
4. **One CTA per post.** "Stream it AND share it AND download the devotional AND follow us" is four CTAs. Pick one.
5. **Respond before you post again.** If the previous post has unanswered comments, respond to them before creating new content. Relationships first.
6. **If you would not want your theology reviewer to see it, do not post it.** This is the brand integrity test.
