## WHAT NOT TO DO ON FRIDAY

- Do NOT refresh your Spotify stream count every 20 minutes — it is discouraging and unproductive
- Do NOT compare your launch day numbers to major label artists — they have marketing budgets of hundreds of thousands of dollars
- Do NOT post more than 4–5 times across all platforms — quality and meaning over quantity
- Do NOT neglect personal messages in favor of broadcast posts — one personal message is worth more than ten broadcasts on launch day
- Do NOT make the day only about metrics — this is also a spiritual milestone and deserves to be experienced as one

---

## THE STANDARD FOR SUCCESS ON FRIDAY

This is the first time MusicWorks has run a real album launch. Success is not defined by stream count on Day 1.

**Success on Friday is defined as:**
- The album is live and accessible on all platforms with no technical errors
- At least one person tells you the music moved them spiritually
- The first email had an open rate above 25% (industry standard for independent artists is 20–30%)
- At least one playlist curator responds to the outreach (even to decline)
- The founder goes to sleep on Friday having done everything that was in their control

**Everything else is data.** The data improves with every release. The spiritual impact of the first release cannot be measured on launch day.
