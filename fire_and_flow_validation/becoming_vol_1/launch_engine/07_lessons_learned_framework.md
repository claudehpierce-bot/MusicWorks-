# Lessons Learned Framework
**Project:** Becoming Vol. 1 — Fire & Flow Gospel
**Document:** Deliverable 7 of 9
**Version:** 1.0

---

> The Lessons Learned document is one of the most valuable assets MusicWorks produces. It is the only document that directly improves the next release. Fill it in honestly — not optimistically. A sanitized Lessons Learned document is useless. An honest one is priceless.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS FRAMEWORK

This document has three parts:

1. **Running Notes** — observations recorded throughout the campaign as things happen. Do not wait until Day 90 to start writing. Record in real time.

2. **90-Day Formal Review** — a structured, comprehensive debrief completed on Day 90 (approximately October 1, 2026). Pull your KPI dashboard data and answer every question.

3. **Feed-Forward Summary** — a one-page distillation of the most important lessons, written after the review, filed in a location where it will be read at the start of the next project. This is what feeds into Agent 01 (Market Signal Researcher) for the next release.

---

## PART 1: RUNNING NOTES (Record Throughout Campaign)

*Add entries to this section whenever something notable happens. Date every entry.*

### Format for Running Notes
```
[DATE]: [What happened] | [Why it matters] | [What to do differently or repeat]

Example:
[July 3, 2026]: The launch email had a 38% open rate on launch day —
higher than expected. | The subject line "It's here." performed better than
the longer subject line we tested previously. | Use short, emotional subject
lines for high-stakes emails. Avoid explanatory subject lines.
```
