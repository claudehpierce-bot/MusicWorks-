## LAUNCH PHASE (Launch Day → Day 7)

---

### LAUNCH DAY — FRIDAY, JULY 3, 2026

**12:00 AM:** Album goes live — verify on all platforms
**8:00 AM:** Launch email sends (automated)
**8:30 AM:** Primary launch social post — all platforms
**10:00 AM:** YouTube video live (or share link if already scheduled)
**12:00 PM:** Midday check — streams, opens, engagement
**2:00 PM:** Behind the Song post — go deeper on one track
**6:00 PM:** Evening gratitude post — personal and warm
**9:00 PM:** End of Day 1 review — write notes in Lessons Learned

**Launch Day Success Benchmarks:**
- Spotify streams: 50–200 (realistic for a new independent artist on Day 1)
- YouTube views: 25–150
- Email open rate: 25%+ 
- Devotional downloads: 10+
- Personal "thank you" messages received: 5+

### DAYS 1–7 (July 4–10, 2026) — Launch Week

**Daily actions:**
- [ ] Check Spotify for Artists each morning (streams, saves, listener geography)
- [ ] Respond to every comment on every platform — same day
- [ ] Respond to every DM about the album — same day
- [ ] One piece of content per day (rotating: Scripture / Behind the Song / Listener feature / Ministry)

**Weekly actions:**
- [ ] Send Email 4 (Scripture Deep Dive) — Day 3 after launch (July 6)
- [ ] Track 2 feature post/video
- [ ] Check playlist curator responses — follow up if no response within 5 days

**What to watch:**
- Which track is getting the most streams? That is your next push track.
- Which platform is growing fastest? Double your time there.
- Is anyone sharing the album organically? Those people are your ambassadors — acknowledge them.
