# DS-1 — Luminous Design™

**Status:** Founder-approved constitutional standard
**Document ID:** DS-1
**Version:** 1.0
**Approved:** 2026-07-14
**Scope:** MusicWorks™, and — per the Founder direction establishing this
document — all future MindSpark products.

---

## Governance

This document is governed the same way MusicWorks' other constitutional
material is governed (see `brand/registry/asset_registry.json` for the
identity-asset equivalent):

- **Single source of truth.** DS-1's content lives only here. Implementation
  plans, milestone specs, and other documents must **reference** this file
  (e.g. "per DS-1") rather than copy its principles inline. Copies drift;
  references don't.
- **Founder-approved revisions only.** Nothing in this document changes
  without explicit Founder approval, recorded as a new entry in the
  Revision History section at the bottom — never a silent edit.
- **Immutable history.** Past revisions are never deleted or rewritten, in
  this file's Revision History or in git history. A change supersedes; it
  does not erase.
- **Referenced, not duplicated.** Future UI work — Sage Presence™, the
  Boardroom, Campaign Operations, MusicWorks Home, and future MindSpark
  products — is expected to cite this document as the visual constitution
  it implements against.

No implementation changes are made or implied by this document's creation.
It establishes the standard; it does not itself build anything.

## Mission

MusicWorks must never feel like software.

It must feel like entering a world where creative excellence already exists.

The interface should reduce anxiety, reward curiosity, and inspire
confidence before the founder creates a single asset.

Beauty is not decoration.

Beauty is trust.

---

## Core Principle

Every screen should communicate calm confidence.

Motion exists to guide attention. Never to demand it.

Color exists to establish atmosphere. Never to entertain.

Light exists to communicate life. Never spectacle.

---

## The Living Interface

Nothing important should feel completely lifeless.

Everything important should feel quietly alive.

Examples include:

- slow ambient gradients
- breathing illumination
- subtle depth
- restrained particle movement
- elegant waveform animation
- soft transitions
- gentle lighting changes

Never excessive animation. Never visual noise.

---

## The Luminous Palette

Base colors:

- Midnight Navy
- Deep Charcoal
- Warm Cream
- Brushed Gold
- Forest Green
- Soft Copper
- Warm White

Accent colors should derive naturally from the campaign artwork. The
interface should quietly inherit the mood of the creator's project.

---

## Movement

Every animation must answer one question:

> "What does this help the founder understand?"

**Allowed motion:** fade, illuminate, reveal, write, breathe, float, settle.

**Avoid:** bounce, shake, spin, explode, unnecessary particle storms,
distracting transitions.

---

## Sage™

Sage is the emotional anchor.

She remains still. The world around her breathes.

Presence comes from: soft light, waveform, voice, transcript, calm movement.

Never facial animation. Never exaggerated gestures.

*(Sage's visual identity itself — the canonical portrait — is governed
separately as a constitutional identity asset; see
`brand/registry/asset_registry.json`, `SAGE-AVATAR-1`. DS-1 governs how she
is presented in motion and light; it does not govern what she looks like.)*

---

## The Boardroom

The Boardroom should feel prestigious. Like entering an executive meeting.

As departments complete work:

- the room quietly brightens
- documents appear
- status settles
- confidence grows

Nothing celebrates loudly.

---

## Creative Work

Generated assets should appear intentionally. Never pop into existence.

They should arrive — almost like being placed onto a polished desk.

---

## Sound

Silence is part of the experience.

Every sound should have purpose: paper, brush, camera, keyboard, wood, one
orchestral confirmation. Nothing else.

---

## Light

MusicWorks should become recognizable through light. Not logos. Not effects.

Light. Soft gold. Soft depth. Subtle reflections. Quiet confidence.

---

## National Geographic Principle

Authenticity over perfection.

Natural over artificial.

Depth over saturation.

Emotion over spectacle.

---

## Apple Principle

Remove before adding.

Refine before decorating.

Premium products require restraint.

---

## Founder Test

Before approving any interface element, ask:

- Does this reduce stress?
- Does this increase confidence?
- Does this teach something?
- Does this make creativity easier?

If not, it does not belong.

---

## Final Standard

MusicWorks should not look impressive. It should feel unforgettable.

The creator is the hero. The agency is alive. The interface simply lets
both shine.
