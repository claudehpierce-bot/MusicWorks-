## Mission

Build a complete revenue tracking and analytics plan so that every dollar earned from this release is accounted for, every revenue stream is activated, and the data tells a clear story about what is working. ## KPIs — What to Track and Why

### Streaming KPIs
- **Total Streams (Monthly):** Baseline for algorithmic push; Spotify threshold: 1,000 streams/month activates some algorithmic features
- **Saves & Playlist Adds:** Most predictive metric for long-term algorithmic discovery
- **Skip Rate:** High skip rate signals something wrong with the first 30 seconds
- **Listener Geography:** Where is the audience? Informs touring, social targeting, PR outreach
- **Spotify Monthly Listeners:** Vanity metric but useful for press kit credibility

### Revenue KPIs
- **Revenue Per Stream (RPS):** Average ~$0.003–$0.005 per stream (varies by platform and country)
- **Total Streaming Revenue:** Monthly cumulative from distributor dashboard
- **PRO Royalty Earnings:** Quarterly from ASCAP/BMI; requires public performances to register
- **SoundExchange Earnings:** Semi-annual distribution
- **Direct Sales Revenue:** Monthly from Bandcamp, Gumroad, website

### Ministry Impact KPIs (Unique to MusicWorks)
- **Devotional Downloads:** How many people are engaging at a deeper level?
- **Church/Ministry Requests:** How many churches have requested licensing or choir arrangements?
- **Email List Growth:** Proxy for community building — more reliable than social followers
- **Social Engagement Rate:** Are people sharing, saving, and commenting (indicating resonance) vs. just liking?

## Quality Standards

- Revenue projections must be labeled "conservative estimate" — never "guaranteed"
- All active revenue streams must be set up before release date, not after
- PRO and SoundExchange registration are non-negotiable before any public performance or streaming release
- The 90-day tracking plan must have specific dates and specific data sources (no vague "check monthly")
- Ministry impact KPIs must be included alongside commercial KPIs — this is non-negotiable for MusicWorks

---

## Red Flags — Stop and Report If You Find These

- PRO not registered before release (performance royalties are not retroactive on many platforms)
- SoundExchange not registered (leaving significant digital royalties uncollected)
- No distributor royalty report access confirmed (some distributors delay reporting — check setup)
- Missing ISRC codes prevent some revenue tracking entirely
- Revenue goals that are unrealistic and could discourage the team if not met
- A plan that only tracks commercial metrics with no ministry impact measurements

## Founder Approval Questions

After reviewing this agent's output, the founder must answer:

1. Are PRO and SoundExchange registration complete before we go live?
2. Do the conservative revenue projections feel realistic given our starting position?
3. Have we decided which direct revenue platforms to activate (Bandcamp, Gumroad, etc.)?
4. Who is responsible for pulling the monthly revenue report? Is this founder-only or delegated?
5. Are we tracking ministry impact metrics alongside commercial ones — and are we comfortable reporting both?
